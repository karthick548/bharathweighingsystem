Who We Are

Established in 2012 at Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Bharath Weighing System is recognised as a leading manufacturer, trader, distributor, wholesaler, supplier and service provider of Weighing Machines & Weights.

We are engaged in trading, manufacturing, wholesaling, distributing and supplying of Weighing Machines & Weights. We also offer Health Weighing Scales, Test Weights and Measuring Weights for both residential and commercial use.

All products are designed for easy weight readability and are inspected post-production on quality parameters. We procure from reputed vendors using the finest raw materials and latest technology.

We also provide electronic weighing machine rental services and undertake all types of weighing scale service & repair across Chennai.
